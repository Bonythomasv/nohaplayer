# NOHA Player Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** (update when you publish)

NOHA Player is an IPTV client. It does not provide or host any media content. Users load their own playlists. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## Data We Handle
- **User-provided playlists and channel selections**: stored locally on your device.
- **Favorites, recents, hidden channels, parental PIN, settings (e.g., autoplay, start on boot)**: stored locally on your device.
- We do **not** send this data to our servers and do **not** use analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Permissions We Use
- **INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: stream user-supplied IPTV content and check connectivity.
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: optional start-on-boot feature (user-controlled in settings).

## Children
The app is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app if you are under 13.

## Third-Party Content
All content is user-supplied via playlists. You are responsible for ensuring you have rights to any content you stream.

## Data Sharing
We do not share your data with third parties.

## Security
Data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, locally stored data is removed.

## Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact us via the email listed on the Google Play store listing for NOHA Player.
